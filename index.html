Too few young women choose to pursue an Engineering degree at University. This is a problem! Lack of knowledge about what these degrees consist of, outdated norms connected to tech, and a perception that it is too difficult are factors that matter. This has over the years created a large deficit of female engineers. This is something that the Womengineer foundation wants to change.

This site is created to give you a better picture of the gender gap today and of how girls and women are represented at High School, in Higher Education, and at the Labor Market.

The lack of female engineers is not only an individual problem, it is a problem for the entire society. Less innovations are created when everyone thinks alike, non gender equal companies are less profitable, and the wage gap between men and women never ceases to exist.
