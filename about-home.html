About

We are glad to see that you have found your way to our site. This site aims to give you a better understanding of the situation in Sweden for girls and women in STEM. We cover areas from girls in pre highschool age interests in STEM to wage and equality questions within the STEM labour markets. We have tried to make it as approachable as possible by presenting most of the data in a graphical format. All the data is available under Data Visualization in the menu.

The site is produced for Womengineer by Youn Hee Pernling Frödin. To learn more about Womengineer and Youn Hee please read more on dedicated pages under About.

Please visit our FAQ to get an answer to the most common questions around Womengineer and girls and women in STEM.
