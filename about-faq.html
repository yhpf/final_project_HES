As of spring 2020, 35 percent of the students in Sweden that graduated with a Master of Science (in any of the Engineering fields) were women. Womengineers goal is that this number should be 50 percent by the year 2030.

In 2019 one in four poeple that worked in a profession relatd to a Master of Science in Engineering was a women. However, the number of women from different specializations differs a lot. For example, in the field of Biotech women are well represented, and in the Computer Science field less so.
