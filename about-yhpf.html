About yhpf

The site was created by Youn Hee Pernling Frödin, and it was created for Womengineer and as a final project in the course Fundamentals of Website Development at Harvard Extension School.

Youn Hee Pernling Frödin

Youn Hee is a Swede living in Brooklyn, New York. She is an Economist that always worked with data and she is a Data Analyst. Lately she has also become an aspiring Front End Developer. At the moment Youn Hee is pursuing a Graduate Certificate in Front End Web Development at Harvard Extension School and she is the Web Developer and Scrum Master for Womengineers website and web team.
